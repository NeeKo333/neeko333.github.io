FAQ

How do I choose the right plastic surgeon?
Research the surgeon's qualifications and experience, check for certification, and membership in professional associations. Read patient reviews and examine before-and-after photos of previous procedures.
What are the risks associated with plastic surgery?
Any surgical procedure carries risks, such as infections, bleeding, and reactions to anesthesia. Thoroughly assess the patient's health and follow the doctor's post-operative recommendations to minimize risks.
How long is the recovery time after plastic surgery?
Recovery time varies depending on the type of surgery. Some procedures may only require a few days to return to normal activities, while others may take weeks or even months. The doctor will provide an individualized recovery plan.
How long do the results of plastic surgery last?
The longevity of results depends on factors such as the type of surgery, the patient's age, lifestyle, and genetics. Well-performed procedures typically have long-lasting effects, but natural aging processes can still impact results.
